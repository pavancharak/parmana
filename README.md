# Parmana

> **Human Authority for Enterprise AI**

## Execution Authorization and Verification Infrastructure for Enterprise AI

### Parmana ensures AI executes only policy-compliant actions.

Parmana is an **Execution Authorization and Verification Infrastructure** that enables organizations to confidently deploy Enterprise AI into high-impact business workflows.

It evaluates every AI execution request against organizational policy before execution, authorizes only policy-compliant actions, and produces cryptographically verifiable execution evidence that can be independently verified long after execution.

Unlike AI orchestration platforms that focus on what AI can do, Parmana focuses on **what AI is allowed to do**.

Parmana enables organizations to deploy Enterprise AI with confidence by ensuring that execution is:

* Authorized
* Policy-compliant
* Deterministic
* Auditable
* Independently verifiable

---

# The Problem

Enterprise AI is rapidly moving from assisting employees to performing business operations.

Modern AI agents can:

* Approve payments
* Provision infrastructure
* Access enterprise systems
* Execute financial operations
* Update customer records
* Coordinate multi-step workflows

As AI capabilities increase, organizations face a fundamental challenge:

**How can Enterprise AI be trusted to execute real business operations?**

Traditional AI infrastructure provides excellent capabilities for:

* reasoning
* planning
* orchestration
* tool calling

But it rarely answers the questions that matter most to enterprise risk, compliance, and governance teams:

* Was this action authorized?
* Which policy approved it?
* Which trusted information was evaluated?
* Why was execution permitted?
* Can the execution be independently verified?
* Can the organization prove compliance months or years later?

Without trustworthy execution authorization, Enterprise AI cannot safely operate in regulated or mission-critical environments.

---

# The Solution

Parmana introduces an independent execution authorization layer between Enterprise AI and enterprise systems.

Instead of allowing AI systems to execute actions directly, Parmana evaluates every execution request before execution occurs.

If organizational policy authorizes the request:

→ execution proceeds.

If policy rejects the request:

→ execution is prevented.

Every authorization produces:

* a deterministic authorization decision,
* an immutable Execution Trust Record,
* cryptographically verifiable execution evidence,
* and a signed receipt for independent verification.

The result is Enterprise AI that organizations can trust to perform real work.

# Trusted Execution Signals

Parmana evaluates execution requests using **trusted execution signals** rather than trusting AI assertions alone.

Execution signals are classified into three categories.

## Enterprise Facts

Enterprise Facts are deterministic signals obtained from trusted systems of record.

Examples include:

* ERP systems
* IAM platforms
* Finance systems
* HR systems
* Policy registries
* Approval systems
* Identity providers

Enterprise Facts represent authoritative business truth.

---

## AI-Derived Signals

AI-Derived Signals are generated by AI models during evaluation.

Examples include:

* intent classification
* document classification
* fraud detection
* risk scoring
* confidence estimation
* semantic analysis

These signals are evaluated together with their confidence, provenance, freshness, and reliability.

---

## External Signals

External Signals originate outside the enterprise.

Examples include:

* regulatory services
* government systems
* compliance providers
* credit bureaus
* external APIs
* sanctions databases

Policies determine how these signals contribute to authorization decisions.

# Core Principles

Parmana is built on five foundational principles.

## 1. Authorization Before Execution

Execution never occurs before policy evaluation.

---

## 2. Human Authority

Enterprise AI may recommend or request actions.

Organizations—not AI—determine what is authorized through policy.

---

## 3. Trusted Execution Signals

Authorization decisions are based on trusted enterprise information rather than AI assertions alone.

---

## 4. Independent Verification

Execution evidence can be verified independently without trusting the original runtime.

---

## 5. Cryptographic Trust

Every execution produces cryptographically verifiable evidence that preserves integrity, authenticity, and long-term auditability.

# Core Concepts

## Authority

The human, organization, or delegated authority permitted to authorize execution.

---

## Authorization

Authorization determines whether a requested execution is permitted under organizational policy.

Authorization establishes **who may authorize execution**.

---

## Intent

Intent represents the requested business operation.

Intent describes **what** Enterprise AI wants to perform independently of implementation.

---

## Policy Reference

A versioned reference to the organizational policy governing execution.

Policies are immutable, deterministic, and independently auditable.

---

## Decision

A Decision is the deterministic result of evaluating an execution request against policy.

A Decision records:

* evaluated policy
* trusted execution signals
* decision outcome
* decision reasoning
* evaluation timestamp

A Decision authorizes execution but never performs execution.
